Data Cleaning in SQL

Summary: This project analyzes a layoff dataset to uncover trends, patterns, and key factors driving workforce reductions across industries. I chose this dataset to gain insights into a timely and impactful topic, showcasing my ability to interpret data and address real-world challenges relevant to business decision-making.

Objective:: The objective of data cleaning in SQL is to ensure the dataset's accuracy, completeness, and reliability by identifying and correcting errors, inconsistencies, and missing values. This process improves the quality of the data, making it suitable for analysis and enabling more accurate and insightful results. By cleaning data, I can avoid skewed findings and ensure that decisions are based on sound, trustworthy information.

Data source: link to dataset.

Process: To clean data in SQL, it is important to identify and remove duplicate records to ensure each entry is unique. Then, handle missing values by either filling them in with appropriate data or deleting incomplete rows or columns. Finally, I corrected any inconsistencies or errors in the data, such as fixing typos and standardizing formats, to maintain data integrity and accuracy.
